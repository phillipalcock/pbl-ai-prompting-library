# Lesson Planning

This module covers how to design, structure, and sequence lessons that support meaningful learning within and beyond project-based learning. Whether you are building lessons for a PBL unit or a more traditional sequence, the principles here will help you plan instruction that is purposeful, engaging, and responsive to student needs.

## What You'll Learn

- Structuring effective lessons using proven instructional frameworks
- Sequencing instruction logically within and across lessons
- Designing engaging learning activities that match your objectives
- Differentiating instruction for diverse learners
- Using AI to generate and refine lesson plans
