# edu-workflows.ai

A practical guide for educators who want to design rigorous, engaging curriculum using AI tools.

## What You'll Learn

This guide walks you through every stage of curriculum design — from unpacking standards to building student materials — with AI as your co-designer. Each section includes templates, examples, prompts, and exercises you can use immediately.

## Who This Is For

- Teachers looking to save time on curriculum development
- Educators who want to use AI tools effectively and responsibly
- Curriculum designers building resources at scale
- Instructional coaches supporting teachers with AI integration

## How to Use This Site

Work through the sections in order, or jump to the topic you need. Each section is self-contained but builds on concepts from earlier parts.

Use the **Templates** section for ready-to-use planning documents. Check **Examples** for complete unit designs you can reference or adapt.
