## What's Included

This template guides you through:

1. **Unit Identity** — Title, subject, grade level, duration, and standards addressed
2. **Essential Questions** — Open-ended questions that frame the unit's inquiry
3. **Learning Objectives** — Measurable outcomes aligned to standards
4. **Assessment Plan** — Formative and summative assessments with criteria
5. **Lesson Sequence** — Day-by-day or lesson-by-lesson progression
6. **Materials List** — Resources, tools, texts, and technology needed
7. **Differentiation Plan** — Accommodations, modifications, and extensions
8. **Implementation Notes** — Scheduling, grouping strategies, and logistics

## How to Use

1. Make a copy of the template document
2. Start with Unit Identity and the standards you need to address
3. Draft essential questions that connect standards to student inquiry
4. Write learning objectives aligned to your selected standards
5. Plan assessments that measure each objective
6. Sequence lessons to build toward assessment readiness
7. Identify materials and plan differentiation strategies
8. Use AI prompts to generate draft content for any section, then review and revise

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
Start with your standards and work forward through the template. Each section builds on the previous one, so completing them in order ensures alignment across the entire unit.
</Callout>
