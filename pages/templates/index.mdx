# Templates

Ready-to-use planning documents for your curriculum design process. Each template includes instructions and can be adapted to any subject or grade level.

## Available Templates

- **Unit Planning Template** — Plan a complete instructional unit from standards to assessments and lesson sequence
- **Lesson Plan Template** — Design individual lessons with clear structure, objectives, and differentiation
- **Assessment Alignment Grid** — Map standards to objectives to assessments across a unit
- **Rubric Builder Template** — Create clear, criteria-based rubrics with defined performance levels
- **Standards Unpacking Worksheet** — Deconstruct curriculum standards into knowledge, skills, and cognitive demands
