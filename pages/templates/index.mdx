# Templates

Ready-to-use planning documents for your PBL design process. Each template includes instructions and can be adapted to any subject or grade level.

## Available Templates

- **Mission Planning Template** — Plan your complete PBL mission from driving question to final product
- **18-Page Workbook Structure** — The full workbook framework with page-by-page descriptions
- **Student Role Creator** — Design authentic student roles with responsibilities and deliverables
- **Assessment Alignment Grid** — Map standards to goals to assessments
- **Standard Transformation Worksheet** — Convert curriculum standards into PBL-ready objectives
