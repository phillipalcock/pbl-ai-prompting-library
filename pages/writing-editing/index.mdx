# Writing & Editing

This module covers how to write clear, effective educational content and edit AI-generated text for your curriculum materials.

Whether you are building lesson plans, student handouts, project guides, or assessment instructions, strong writing and careful editing ensure that your materials communicate exactly what learners need.

## What You'll Learn

- **Writing clear educational content** — plain language principles, structuring instructions, and writing for different audiences
- **Editing AI-generated text** — identifying common AI writing issues and applying a structured review process
- **Managing reading level and tone** — assessing readability, adjusting for grade bands, and maintaining a consistent voice
- **A comprehensive editing checklist** — a step-by-step quality check for any curriculum document
